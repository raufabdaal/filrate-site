Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly via our contact forms, including name, practice name, email address, phone number, and message content. We also collect anonymized usage data through standard server logs to improve site performance and security.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, schedule onboarding calls, deliver our revenue recovery services, and communicate product updates. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Patient Health Information (PHI)

Filrate operates as a Business Associate under HIPAA when interfacing with covered entities. Any PHI accessed through practice management system integrations is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest, stored on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, and only used for the specific operational recovery functions authorized by your practice. We sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with every partner before accessing PHI.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality. We do not employ third-party advertising trackers or behavioral remarketing pixels.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact information for as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us.

6. Data Transfers

Filrate is operated globally. Your data may be processed on secure servers located in the United States or other jurisdictions with adequate data protection standards. All transfers use industry-standard encryption.

7. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data access requests, or deletion requests, contact us at zinduro@gmail.com.
