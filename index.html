Founder Profile

Built from the operatory. Not the boardroom.

Filrate was not conceived in a technology incubator or a corporate boardroom. It was forged directly in the operational friction of a real high-volume practice: Dino Dental Kampala, a multi-operatory clinic serving patients daily.

Working inside that practice, our founder watched clinical personnel struggle under administrative load. Answering patient recall inquiries, chasing late cancellations, tracking outstanding treatment plan values from the database ledger. Every day, front desk teams were expected to perform manual miracles to keep chairs full, while thousands in uncollected revenues drifted away.

"We realized that the solution was not another software portal that required more manual hours from your staff. It had to be a silent, autonomous infrastructure layer, operating invisibly to plug these operational gaps in real-time."

Clinical Lead Kasajja Abdurauf, Founder

+

Zero. Filrate operates as a fully managed infrastructure layer. It works autonomously in the background without adding a single task to your administrative workflow.

What is the timeline from the audit to active deployment?

+

Full configuration and secure system integration are completed within 14 business days from your initial Revenue Leak Audit.

Is Filrate going to sound robotic to my patients?

+

No. Adam uses our proprietary clinical-grade voice engine. He speaks with natural cadence, pauses, and empathy. Patients regularly compliment front desks on "the new hire's" professional phone manner.

None. Filrate routes autonomously through your existing telephony provider (RingCentral, Weave, Mango, etc.) and connects directly via API/webhook logic to your software.
